# Red Lentil Dal with Cherry Tomatoes and Spinach

Prepration time: 20 minutes
Cooking time: 30 minutes
Serves: 4
Calories: 


## Ingredients

1 white onion
1 cup red lentils
1 tbsp olive oil
1/2 bulb garlic
1 medium carrot, diced
cherry tomatoes
6-8 balls of frozen spinach
boullion powder
1 tin (200g) chopped tomatoes
rice or (easier) couscous
2 tsp garam masala
fenugrek
fresh coriander


## Method

1. Heat olive oil and fry onions and garlic for 3 minutes.
2. Add lentils, vegetable stock, cherry tomatoes, carrots, and bring to simmer.
3. Stir in garam masala, fenugrek
4. Leave to simmer, stirring occasionally, for 20 minutes.
5. Stir in spinach until defrosted.
6. Simmer for another 10 minutes while preparing rice/couscous.
7. Serve with a garnish of coriander.
